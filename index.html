What is

YHack '26?

Y Hack '26 is a National Level 24-Hour Offline Hackathon organized by IEEE in association with the Department of Mechatronics, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology. Designed as an open innovation platform, Y Hack brings together students, professionals, researchers, and innovators from across India to develop impactful solutions to real-world challenges.

With dedicated Hardware and Software tracks, participants will transform ideas into functional prototypes while experiencing an intense 24-hour innovation journey.

Eligibility & Rules

Open to all disciplines. Team size: 3–6 members (cross-college not permitted). All submissions must be original and adhere to IEEE's Code of Conduct.

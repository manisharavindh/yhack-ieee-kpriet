What is

YHack?

YHack is the annual technical event by IEEE RAS KPRIET that brings together the brightest minds to build, break, learn and lead the change. Open to all domains — engineering, design, research, and beyond.

About the Department

The Department at KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology fosters a culture of technical excellence and innovation. With state-of-the-art labs, industry partnerships, and a vibrant student community, our department is the breeding ground for next-generation engineers and creators.

02

About IEEE RAS

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) Student Chapter at KPRIET is dedicated to advancing innovation in robotics, automation, and intelligent systems. We organize workshops, hackathons, and technical events that empower students to push boundaries and build impactful solutions.

Everything You Need to Know

DETAILS

Who Can Participate?

YHack is open to all domains — engineering, arts, science, management. No restrictions.

  • Undergraduate & Postgraduate students
  • Any college, any branch, any year
  • Teams of exactly 3 members
  • Cross-college teams are welcome

Problem Statement Domains

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Web3 & Blockchain
  • IoT & Embedded Systems
  • Sustainable Technology
  • HealthTech & BioTech
  • Open Innovation (any domain)

Rules & Regulations

  • Teams must consist of exactly 3 members
  • All code must be written during the event
  • Pre-built projects are not allowed
  • Use of open-source libraries is permitted
  • Judges' decision is final and binding
  • Respectful conduct is mandatory
  • Plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification

Registration Info

  • Register all 3 team members with full details
  • Name, Phone, Department, Year, College
  • Accommodation costs auto-calculated
  • Payment confirmation via email
  • WhatsApp group link provided after registration
  • Late registrations will NOT be accepted
